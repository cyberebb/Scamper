Scamper

This page is about my beloved dog, Scamper. Scamper was a very playful Shih-Tzu that I had for 17 years growing up.


These were some of Scamper's favorite activities to do during the day.

  1. Go for walks
  2. Play with his favorite toys:
    • Frisbees
    • Tennis Balls
    • Squeaky Toys
  3. Chew on dental bones

Scamper was a quiet dog, except when it came time to feed him every morning and afternoon.
